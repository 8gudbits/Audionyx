# Audionyx

An app that lets you listen remotely to what's happening around your phone from your Bluetooth headphones.

## What It Does

- Turns your phone into a remote microphone
- Streams audio in real-time to your Bluetooth headphones
- Perfect for monitoring a room, baby, or pets while you're elsewhere
- Works with any Bluetooth headphones or speakers

## How to Use

1. Place your phone in the area you want to monitor
2. Connect your Bluetooth headphones to your phone
3. Open Audionyx and tap the microphone button
4. You'll hear audio from your phone's location through your headphones

## Features

- Real-time audio streaming to Bluetooth devices
- Simple one-touch operation
